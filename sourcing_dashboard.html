Rejection Reasons

Overall Rejection Reasons
No response (38%) is the #1 rejection reason by a wide margin — across every role and seniority. This is a sourcing and engagement problem, not an evaluation problem.

Actionable Conclusions by Rejection Reason
Rejection ReasonWhere it Hurts MostRecommendation
No response People BP (43%), Talent Acquisition (44%) Improve first contact personalization for these roles. One follow-up max.
Salary mismatch Analista de Dados (6.6%), Senior profiles Align salary expectations at screening — don't wait for offer stage.
Accepted another offer Junior (5.8%), Talent Acquisition (5%) Reduce time to offer for competitive profiles — process speed matters.
Timing mismatch Analista de Recrutamento (6.2%), Pleno Ask about availability and notice period at first contact.
Headcount closed Staff (5.8%) Avoid opening senior roles without confirmed headcount approval.
Low engagement People BP (5.3%) People BP profiles require higher-touch sourcing — cold outreach may not work.
